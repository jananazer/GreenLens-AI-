# GreenLens-AI-
Platform designed to help business leaders monitor the environmental and operational impact of their company’s AI usage by tracking metrics such as carbon emissions, water consumption, and license utilization to identify cost-saving opportunities. Awarded 1st place overall and Cleanest Code by Sun Life judges.
